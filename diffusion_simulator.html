3 Models of Diffusion

Innovation Adoption Simulator
Population Parameters
Broadcast

Mass advertising (e.g., Super Bowl ads). Everyone in the remaining population sees the message each period — diffusion is proportional to the number of non-adopters.

Contagion

Word of mouth. Each existing customer "infects" others — diffusion is proportional to current adopters. Self-sustaining once it starts; no ongoing investment required.

Outreach

Direct sales, cold outreach, or fixed ad spend. A set number of new customers are added each period through deliberate effort.

+ Reinvestment Rate

Profits from each existing customer are reinvested into outreach. Unlike contagion, this requires an active decision to keep spending — it scales your fixed outreach by the size of your current customer base.
